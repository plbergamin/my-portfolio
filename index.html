Architecture - Selected Projects

House in São Paulo

House in Brazil

This house is designed with a focus on the beautiful southern view that the plot offers. To fully embrace this connection, the residence is conceived as a series of small, asymmetrical boxes, supported by pillars, which create all the necessary living spaces in the most open and transparent way possible. These "boxes," which house the bedrooms, bathrooms, and garage, are completely white, featuring large windows, and are elevated above street level, resulting in an intriguing visual effect. The living room is enclosed in a glass cube, while the service areas are concealed within the natural topography.

Apartment buildings in Maceió

Apartment Building in Maceió

The project stemmed from a fundamental concept of designing vertical houses that offer uninterrupted views of a captivating 15-meter seafront. Emphasizing a seamless integration with the natural landscape, theproject ensures an immersive experience for its residents. However, what sets this project apart is a visionary immediate that extends beyond the immediate site boundaries. Recognizing the potential of the expanding ingeniously city area, the project incorporates harmonious adjacent land to create a fusion of private dwellings and high-quality commercial services and public spaces.

Office Building in Germany

Office Building in Germany

This project is an extension of a previously constructed office building. The new design focuses on enhancing functionality and flexibility, incorporating modern open workspace concepts that promote collaboration and efficiency. The main façade has been reimagined to reflect a contemporary aesthetic, while the floorplan has been carefully organized to accommodate a greater number of workstations. In addition to the open work areas, the design includes well-appointed meeting rooms, rest areas, and technical rooms, ensuring a balanced environment that supports both productivity and employee well-being.
